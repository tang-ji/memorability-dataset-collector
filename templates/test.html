Tips

After the game start, you will see a series of images. Some of them will be shown twice, so your task is to find them out. Every image will be shown during 1.4 seconds, pay attention but take it easy!

Space mark the current image as "seen" if you think it's not the first time you see it.

P pause the game.

ESC retart the game. (don't use it in the end of game, because you will be confused about what you have already seen)

H for help.

L for leaderboard.
